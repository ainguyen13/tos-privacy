1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application and related services (the “App” or “Service”) operated by us (“we”, “us”, “our”). It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or apps that may be linked from or integrated with the App.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following categories of information:

2.1. Information you provide

  • Contact information (such as name and email) when you contact us or submit a form.
  • Message content you submit (for example, the text you type into a contact form).

2.2. Device and usage information

  • Device information: device type/model, operating system, app version, language settings.
  • Log and diagnostics: timestamps, feature usage, crash reports, performance data.
  • Identifiers (if applicable): device identifiers or similar technical identifiers used for analytics and troubleshooting.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as health, financial account numbers, or biometric identifiers) unless the App specifically requires it and provides appropriate notice/consent mechanisms (if applicable).

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Analyze usage, monitor performance, and fix bugs.
  • Protect the security and integrity of the App and prevent abuse or fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms (if necessary).

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service providers: vendors who help us operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics, crash reporting). They may process information only on our instructions and for the purpose of providing services to us.
  • Legal and safety: to comply with applicable law, lawful requests, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of us, our users, or the public.
  • With your consent: when you explicitly agree to sharing for a specific purpose.

5. Cookies, SDKs, and Analytics

The App may use cookies (on web) or SDKs (on mobile) and similar technologies to:

  • Understand how the App is used (analytics).
  • Improve functionality, user experience, and technical stability.

You may be able to control some tracking through your device or browser settings, depending on your platform and available options.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When we no longer need the information, we will delete or anonymize it using reasonable measures.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information (for example, access controls and encryption in transit where appropriate). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to and a copy of your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of information (in certain circumstances).
  • Object to or restrict processing (in certain circumstances).
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the “Contact” section below.

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

15. Region-Specific Rights

If you are located in the EEA or the UK, processing may be based on: (a) your consent; (b) performance of a contract; (c) compliance with legal obligations; and/or (d) our legitimate interests (such as improving and securing the App), where those interests are not overridden by your rights.

You may have additional rights such as data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

If you are a California resident, you may have rights to know, access, delete, and correct certain personal information, and to opt out of certain “sharing” as defined by California law. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us using the information below.
